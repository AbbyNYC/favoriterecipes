This is my favorite eggplant parmesan recipe. For the sauce get a can of San Marzano tomatoes with basil, blend them with olive oil, garlic and onion then simmer in a pot for 45 minutes. Slice the eggplant and lay in an olive oil lined baking pan, bake for 3 minutes on each side at 350 degrees. Next, in a casserole dish, layer the eggplant between a spoonful of sauce, a slice of mozzarella and fresh grated cheese until you reach almost the top of the casserole. Bake in a 350 degree oven until the cheese starts to melt and the sauce begins to bubble. Let sit for 15-20 minutes before serving.
